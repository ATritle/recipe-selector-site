Bacon Cheeseburger Tatortot Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 3 oz package of real bacon bits (black label)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 32 oz frozen tator tots
  • 10.75 oz can condensed cream of chicken or cheddar cheese soup
  • 16 oz sour cream

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Spray 9x13 pan.
  3. Cook ground beef and drain fat.
  4. In a large bowl mix cream of chicken, sour cream, bacon bits, cheddar cheese, and beef. Then mix in tator tots.
  5. Spread mixture in pan and bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
