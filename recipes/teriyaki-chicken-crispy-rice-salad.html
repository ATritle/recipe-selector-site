Teriyaki Chicken Crispy Rice Salad

Ingredients:

  • CRISPY RICE:
  • 2 cups cooked Jasmine rice
  • 1 TBSP sesame oil
  • 2 TBSP teriyaki sauce
  • CHICKEN:
  • 1 rotisserie chicken
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • SALAD:
  • 1 large english cucumber
  • 1/2 cup red cabbage - finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cilantro - finely chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions - diced
  • 1 red bell pepper - diced
  • 1/2 cup peanuts - chopped
  • CREAMY SESAME GINGER DRESSING:
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 TBSP toasted sesame oil
  • 3 TBSP soy sauce
  • 3 TBSP rice vinegar
  • 2 TBSP maple syrup
  • 2 TBSP cashew butter (or peanut butter)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 TBSP fresh ginger

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Add cooked and cooled rice to the baking sheet.
  4. Toss well with the sesame oil and teriyaki sauce and spread into an even layer.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, tossing half way through.
  6. Toss the chicken with teriyaki sauce and set aside.
  7. Add all of the ingredients to a blender fand blend for about 1 minute until smooth.
  8. Chop/slice all of the ingredients for the salad and add to a large bowl.
  9. Add the shredded chicken and cripsy rice on top.
  10. Pour dressing over the salad. Toss and enjoy.
