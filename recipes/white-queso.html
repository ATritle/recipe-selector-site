White Queso

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lbs White American Cheese
  • 5 oz Evaporated Milk
  • 1 TBSP Unsalted Butter
  • 4 oz Green Chilis
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/8 tsp Paprika
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne Powder

Directions:

  1. Place cheese, evaporated milk & butter in a saucepan over low heat, stirring frequently.
  2. Stir in green chillies, cumin, garlic powder & cayenne pepper.
  3. Add more evaporated milk if you want it thinner.
