Taco Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 LBS ground beef
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 20 oz rotel
  • 1 TBSP taco seasoning
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 oz diced jalapenos or poblanos
  • 15 oz can black beans
  • shredded cheddar cheese - to garnish
  • cilantro - chopped to garnish

Directions:

  1. Brown meat with 1/2 TBSP of taco seasoning in Dutch Oven.
  2. Drain grease off and add cream cheese, rotel, drained & rinsed can of black bean, jalapenos/poblanos & remaining taco seasoning.
  3. Add chicken broth and simmer 20-30 minutes.
  4. Garnish with shredded cheese and cilantro.
