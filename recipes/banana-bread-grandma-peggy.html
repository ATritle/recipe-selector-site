Banana Bread Grandma Peggy

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup butter - softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3-4 blackened bananas - mashed
  • 2 eggs - room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cup flour - sifted
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk or sour milk - (1 cup milk & 2 TBSP vinegar)
  • Optional:
  • chocolate chips
  • walnuts

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add bananas, eggs and vanilla. Dont over mix.
  4. Seperate bowl of mix flour, baking soda and salt.
  5. Alternate slowly flour mixture and buttermilk to banana mixture until well combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips or walnuts.
  7. Pour into greased bread pan and bake for 1-1.5 hours.
  8. If dividing batter into 2 pans, bake for 1 hour.
