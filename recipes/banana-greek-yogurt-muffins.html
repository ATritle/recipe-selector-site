Banana Greek Yogurt Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas - mashed
  • 1 cup greek yogurt
  • 2 eggs - room temp
  • 2 cup rolled quick oats
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°
  2. Spray or line muffin tin.
  3. In a food processor, add bananas, greek yogurt, eggs, quick otas, brown sugar, baking powder and baking soda, until oats are broken down and batter is smooth & creamy.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour batter in lined muffin tin 3/4 way full. Optional to add more chocolate chips on top.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the tops are set & toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Store in air tight container up to 1 week.
