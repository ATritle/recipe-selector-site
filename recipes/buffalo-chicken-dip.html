Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 large Cans of Chicken
  • 1 cup Ranch
  • 3/4 cup Buffalo Sauce
  • 2 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 8 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:

  1. Drain juice out of cans of chicken.
  2. Dump all ingredients into a crockpot on high stirring occasionally until cream cheese is melted.
  3. Reduce heat to warm and serve with tortilla chips.
