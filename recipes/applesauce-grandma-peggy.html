Applesauce Grandma Peggy

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 cup granny smith apples peeled & diced
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 TBSP lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 dash vanilla
  • 1 dash cinnamon
  • 1 dash nutmeg

Directions:

  1. Cook apples in water until tender & turn stove off.
  2. Add sugar, vanilla & cinnamon.
  3. Grandma didn't mash the apples.
  4. Boil in jars for 15 minutes to seal jars.
  6. Use immersion blender to make saucy:
  7. 1 cup applesauce - blended
  8. 1/8 tsp vanilla
  9. 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  10. 1/8 tsp nutmeg
